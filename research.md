# Research

My research spans AI ethics, norms in language models, cultural analytics, and digital tourism studies. I use computational methods to investigate how technology shapes society and culture.

## Research Projects

### Discovering and Attesting Digital Discrimination (DADD)
King's College London, 2019-2022 (EPSRC funded)

Cross-disciplinary collaboration investigating algorithmic bias and discrimination, working with academics and industry partners (Google, AI Club). Published 5 papers and developed interactive tools to surface language biases in AI systems.

[Project Website](https://dadd-project.github.io/)

### REPHRAIN
UK National Research Centre on Privacy, Harm Reduction and Adversarial Influence Online, 2022 (UKRI funded)

Led a project investigating intersectional discrimination in automated systems, developing a novel conceptual framework based on interviews and survey data.

[Project Website](http://rephrain.ac.uk)
